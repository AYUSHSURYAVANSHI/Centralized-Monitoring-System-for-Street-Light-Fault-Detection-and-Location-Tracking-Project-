Centralized Monitoring System for Street Light Fault Detection and Location Tracking

The project's primary goal is to improve energy efficiency in street lighting by utilizing a range of sensor technologies. Daylight sensors will automatically turn off lights during the day to save energy.Object detection technology will enhance safety by increasing light intensity when objects are detected at night. Rain sensors will notify authorities during rainy conditions for proactive monitoring. Additionally, a fault detection system will promptly identify issues, sending alerts for immediate attention. Users can also trigger alerts via a button on the streetlight pole, ensuring swift response. The project depends on integrating light sensors for day and night mode detection, utilizing an automatic control system for light regulation, and providing a manual interface for communication backup. A crucial aspect is the power management system to optimize energy usage. The infrastructure must efficiently manage over 100 lights at once, requiring robust data transmission capabilities. Reliable communication over a 15 km radius is vital. A web application will handle data for fault detection, and a system will organize data by pole numbers and areas for effective monitoring and maintenance.

We're dedicated to creating a solution that merges a application/website and IoT hardware to revolutionize street light maintenance in Indian cities.

Daylight Optimization and Motion Sensors

Our smart street lighting system is equipped with daylight sensors that automatically switch off street lights during daylight hours.This not only optimizes energy consumption but also reduces light pollution.Our system utilizes motion sensors to intelligently adjust the brightness of streetlights according to the available sunlight.This adaptive control not only conserves energy but also enhances safety and comfort.Motion Sensors Will Adjust Brightness Levels Based On Natural Light, Decreasing Intensity In The Morning And Increasing It In The Evening.

Object Detection Technology for Enhanced Safety

Safety is our priority. We employ advanced object detection technology to increase light intensity to 100% when objects are detected. This enhances safety and visibility during nighttime, making our streets secure and well-lit.We offer a web application that serves as the hub for data reception and fault detection, ensuring efficient management and control. Additionally, we've implemented a system to sort and associate data with specific pole numbers and areas for effective monitoring and maintenance.

Rain Sensors and Proactive Monitoring

We've integrated rain sensors into our street lighting infrastructure. These sensors detect rainy conditions,This ensures efficient monitoring and control during adverse weather conditions,contributing to public safety.Our reliable communication system can transmit data from lights in a 15km radius, enabling real-time monitoring and control.A proactive monitoring solution involving rain sensors might involve real-time data collection and analysis to trigger appropriate actions based on weather conditions.

Fault Detection and Rapid Response

Our advanced fault detection system is designed to promptly identify issues with street lights. When a fault is detected, our system sends alerts to controllers, displaying red marks on our web/app interface for immediate attention. This guarantees well-maintained, safe streets.Develop or employ algorithms to analyze data from the sensors.These algorithms should be designed to detect anomalies or faults in the street lights,such as burnt-out bulbs,power irregularities, or unusual activity patterns.

User-Triggered Alerts and Manual Control

Empowering community involvement, our system allows users to trigger alerts by pressing a button on the streetlight pole. This feature notifies controllers and displays red marks on our web/app interface for rapid response.In case of communication lag, a user-friendly button interface provides manual control, ensuring safety even in unforeseen situations.In a street lighting system, combining user-triggered alerts and manual controls can enhance its functionality and responsiveness.
